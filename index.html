Before You Enter

A note to every soul who finds this page

Whether you're one of us — a student of G-11 Emerson — or a curious stranger who somehow stumbled upon this little corner of the internet, you are welcome here. This website was built with nothing but love, late nights, and a whole lot of trial and error.

"We were never just a section. We were a chapter —
the kind you re-read just to feel something again."

What you're about to see is a collection of real moments — the kind that don't make it to formal records but live permanently in the hearts of the people who were there. The chaos of a classroom that somehow became a home. The laughter that echoed through hallways. The friendships built between lessons and recess, between hardships and celebrations.

This isn't a perfect website. It was made by someone who had never written a single line of code before this. But maybe that's exactly what makes it special — it was built by someone who cared enough to try.
